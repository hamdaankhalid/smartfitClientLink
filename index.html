How To Take Pictures

It is very important for pictures to be taken correctly for acurate measurements. Before starting make sure of the following:

  • Please be barefoot
  • Wear tight clothes
  • Have a contrasting background from your clothes that you can stand against

Instructions

  1. Place camera at waist level height.
  2. Place Camera completely straight, without any tilt forward or backward (like flat against a wall)
  3. Click Take Picture button
  4. The camera will have a 10 second countdown
  5. Walk back and make sure you are completely visible head to toe
  6. The camera will click picture after 10 seconds, press confirm to save the image, and redo to retake image.
Follow the above instructions to 3 times inorder to take 3 pictures in the poses shown below.
