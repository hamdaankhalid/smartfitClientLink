How To Take Pictures

It is very important for pictures to be taken correctly for acurate measurements. Before starting make sure of the following:

  • Please be barefoot
  • Wear tight clothes
  • Have a contrasting background from your clothes that you can stand against

Instructions

  1. Place Phone/Laptop camera at waist level height.
  2. Place Phone/Laptop Camera completely straight, without any tilt forward or backward (like flat against a wall)
  3. Click Take Picture button
  4. The camera will have a 10 second countdown before clicking a picture automatically
  5. Walk back and make sure you are completely visible head to toe, and make sure you are in the center of the frame (vertically and horizontally).
  6. The camera will click picture after 10 seconds, press confirm to save the image, and redo to retake image.
Follow the above instructions to 3 times inorder to take 3 pictures in the poses shown below.

Poses to Follow

  1. Front Facing pose: arms to the side, palms facing down, arms stretched out, arms lifted slightly away from body, leg slightly apart.
  2. Side Facing pose : arms to the side, palms facing Inside, arms stretched down, arms touching the body, legs together.
  3. Back Facing Pose : Exact same as front facing pose, just facing backwards.

Please click the arrow on the right to start taking pictures!


Finished!

All the pictures you confirmed were downloaded and then stored in your downloads folder on your phone/laptop . If you have taken all photos as shown in all 3 poses and followed the guidelines, you may now go ahead and share these with your tailor/boutique that forwarded you this link, using your preffered mode of communication.


If you are on your phone you may share via whatsapp following the below steps:
  1. Go to your boutique/tailor's chat, click "+" icon
  2. Click Document
  3. Go to Downloads
  4. Select and Share pictures with "smartfit" in name

If you are on your Laptop you may share via Email following the below steps:
  1. Compose a mail to your tailor/boutique's email address
  2. Click on Attachments
  3. Go to Downloads
  4. Select and Share pictures with "smartfit" in name
